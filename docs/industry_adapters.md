# Industry Adapters / 行业适配器

## 1. Semiconductor / Hardware / Manufacturing
Revenue = Unit Shipments × ASP × Product Mix
Variables: shipments, ASP, product mix, capacity, yield, process node, inventory, customer concentration, supply chain
Must answer: shipment growth source? ASP trend? Product mix upgrade? Inventory health? Customer concentration?

## 2. Software / SaaS / Cloud
Revenue = Customers × ARPU; ARR = Customers × ARPU; Growth = New + Expansion - Churn
Variables: ARR, RPO, NRR, Churn, ARPU, CAC, LTV/CAC, Rule of 40, Gross Margin, SBC, FCF Margin
Must answer: Customer growth? Expansion? Churn? CAC trend? Growth/profit balance? AI disruptor?

## 3. Internet Platform / Ad / E-commerce
Ad Revenue = Users × Time × Ad Load × CPM; E-com Revenue = GMV × Take Rate
Variables: DAU/MAU, ARPU, GMV, Take Rate, Ad Load, CPM, CPC
Must answer: User growth plateau? Monetization room? Advertiser ROI healthy? Regulatory ceiling?

## 4. Consumer / Retail / Luxury
Revenue = Stores × SSSG; SSSG = Traffic × Ticket × Repurchase
Variables: SSSG, Store Count, Traffic, Ticket, Repurchase, Inventory Turnover, Discount Rate, Gross Margin, Brand Momentum
Must answer: Growth from stores/SSS/price/channel? Pricing power? Inventory health? Discount dependency?

## 5. Cyclicals / Energy / Commodities
Revenue = Volume × Price; Profit = Volume × (Price - Cash Cost)
Variables: Commodity Price, Volume, Unit Cost, Cost Curve Position, Inventory Cycle, Supply-Demand Balance
Must answer: Cost curve position? Cycle position? Supply expansion? Inventory phase? Low PE at peak = trap.

## 6. Banks / Insurance / Financials
Bank Profit = IEA × NIM + Non-Interest - Credit Cost - Op Cost - Tax
Insurance Value = NBV + In-Force + Investment Returns
Broker Revenue = Brokerage + IB + AM + Prop Trading + Margin
Variables: NIM, IEA, NPL, Provision Coverage, Credit Cost, ROE, Capital Adequacy, AUM
Must answer: Asset quality? ROE sustainable? Credit cost understated? Capital constraints?

## 7. Pharma / Biotech
Mature Rev = Drug Volume × Price × Penetration
Biotech Value = Peak Sales × PoS × Patent Life × Discount Rate
Variables: Pipeline, Clinical Data, TAM, PoS, Patent Life, Reimbursement
Must answer: Patent cliff? Pipeline real rNPV? Payor pressure? PE meaningless for pre-profit biotech.

## 8. Utilities / REITs / Infrastructure
REITs: NOI = Rental Income - Op Costs; FFO = NI + D&A - Asset Sale Gains
Variables: Occupancy, Rental Growth, NOI, FFO, Cap Rate, Leverage, Payout Ratio, Asset Quality
Must answer: Cash flow stable? Rate risk? Dividend sustainable? Growth secondary, cash flow primary.

## Selection Rule
If >60% revenue from one segment, use that adapter. Otherwise blended approach.

# Market Adapters / 市场适配器

**Purpose:** Standardized analytical focus for each equity market. Every report selects exactly one.

---

## US Equity Adapter / 美国权益适配器

GAAP vs Non-GAAP; SBC dilution; FCF quality; Buybacks; Guidance vs consensus; Beat & Raise; USD rates/T-yields; Sector-relative valuation

## China A-Share Adapter / 中国A股适配器

Policy cycle; Theme rotation; Trading volume concentration; Margin balance; Fund positioning congestion; North-bound flows; Lock-up/sell-down; AR/Inventory/Prepayments; Subsidies; Price limit rules

## Hong Kong Equity Adapter / 香港权益适配器

Liquidity discount; South-bound flows; Dividend yield; Buybacks; H/A discount; Governance discount; China ADR/H-share regulatory risk; Privatization

## Global Markets Adapter (Europe/Japan/EM) / 全球市场适配器（欧洲/日本/新兴市场）

FX impact; Local rates; Accounting differences (IFRS vs GAAP); Tax rates; Geopolitical risk; ADR/GDR structure; Local shareholder return practices

---

## Unified Output / 统一输出

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Market** | Primary exchange / market identifier |
| **Currency** | Reporting & trading currency |
| **Accounting Standard** | GAAP, IFRS, or local standards |
| **Key Market Factor** | Dominant driver for this market |
| **Liquidity Environment** | Bid-ask spread, turnover, depth |
| **Regulatory Risk Level** | Low / Medium / High |

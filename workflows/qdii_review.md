# QDII Review Workflow

## Purpose

Review QDII or cross-border fund exposure with attention to NAV lag, FX, holdings, fee drag, and index exposure.

## Steps

1. Identify fund objective, benchmark, holdings, and NAV calculation lag.
2. Data Auditor Agent separates official NAV, estimated NAV, and market proxy data.
3. Macro & Liquidity Agent reviews FX, rates, and cross-border liquidity.
4. Asset Rotation Agent reviews index and sector proxy strength.
5. Valuation & Margin Agent reviews underlying valuation, not only fund price.
6. Portfolio Execution Agent checks position weight and liquidity constraints.
7. Team Lead Agent outputs conditional review, not automatic subscription or redemption advice.

## Output

- Official data versus proxy data.
- FX and NAV-lag notes.
- Holdings exposure.
- Risk and review triggers.

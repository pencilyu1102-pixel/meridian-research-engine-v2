# News Pulse Workflow

## Purpose

Classify company news, market rumors, policy events, and competitor events without overreacting to headline noise.

## Steps

1. Identify event type: company, regulatory, industry, competitor, macro, or market sentiment.
2. Data Auditor Agent checks whether the source is primary, confirmed, stale, or speculative.
3. Industry Position Agent checks industry-level meaning.
4. Company Fundamental Agent checks business impact.
5. Valuation & Margin Agent checks whether the event affects valuation anchors.
6. Bear Case Agent asks what the market may be mispricing.
7. Team Lead Agent classifies: ignore, monitor, thesis review, or full report update.

## Output

- Event timeline.
- Source tier.
- Business impact.
- Valuation impact.
- Portfolio impact.
- Review trigger.

# Industry Funnel Workflow

## Purpose

Screen an industry from broad universe to a short list of companies worth deeper research.

## Steps

1. Define industry boundary and value chain.
2. Industry Position Agent maps cycle stage and competitive intensity.
3. Asset Rotation Agent checks whether the industry has market support.
4. Company Fundamental Agent filters by quality.
5. Valuation & Margin Agent removes clearly overextended names.
6. Data Auditor Agent flags low-confidence or stale data.
7. Bear Case Agent challenges the shortlist.
8. Team Lead Agent ranks candidates by research priority.

## Output

- Industry map.
- Candidate table.
- Exclusion reasons.
- Top research priorities.

# Buy Checklist Workflow

## Purpose

Prevent a research view from becoming an unreviewed buy action.

## Checklist

- Company judgment is positive and evidence-backed.
- Valuation judgment has an explicit anchor.
- Data Auditor Agent allows the data to enter the conclusion.
- Price Level Engine identifies a non-round-number price zone.
- Portfolio Execution Agent confirms cash, margin, concentration, and drawdown capacity.
- Bear Case Agent did not invalidate the thesis.
- Operation conditions and invalidation conditions are explicit.

## Output

Allowed outputs are conditional research conclusions, not automatic buy instructions.

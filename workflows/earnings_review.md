# Earnings Review Workflow

## Purpose

Review an earnings release with attention to data quality, marginal change, guidance, cash flow, and valuation impact.

## Steps

1. Collect official release, filing, transcript, and presentation.
2. Data Auditor Agent checks timestamp, units, accounting basis, and conflicts.
3. Company Fundamental Agent reviews revenue, margin, cash flow, CapEx, SBC, and management commentary.
4. Valuation & Margin Agent updates normalized EPS or FCF assumptions.
5. Bear Case Agent attacks the market reaction and management narrative.
6. Team Lead Agent decides whether the thesis is upgraded, unchanged, downgraded, or invalidated.

## Required Questions

- Did the quarter change the thesis or only the market mood?
- Which metrics improved or deteriorated on the margin?
- Does guidance change normalized earnings power?
- Does the Price Level Engine need updated anchors?

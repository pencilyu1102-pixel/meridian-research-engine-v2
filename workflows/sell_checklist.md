# Sell Checklist Workflow

## Purpose

Separate valuation trimming, thesis failure, risk control, and emotional selling.

## Checklist

- Has the company thesis failed?
- Has valuation moved above the supported range?
- Is the position too large for the account?
- Is the action reducing base position or trading position?
- Does the Price Level Engine identify a valuation or cost-repair zone?
- What data would make selling wrong?

## Output

The output must state whether the sale is thesis-based, valuation-based, account-based, or tactical.
